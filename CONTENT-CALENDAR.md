# GlassesPedia Content Calendar — 4 Articles/Day, 6 Months

## Strategy
- **Goal:** 720+ articles targeting 13,000+ keywords
- **Frequency:** 4 articles/day (Mon-Fri), 2/day weekends
- **Backlink rule:** Every article has 3 links to charmoptical.ca
- **AI optimization:** Quick Answer box, FAQ schema, Speakable schema, definition-first structure

1. **Lenses** — progressive, bifocal, single vision, photochromic, blue light, high index
2. **Coatings & Treatments** — AR, blue light filter, scratch-resistant, UV, hydrophobic
3. **Frame Styles** — aviator, cat-eye, rimless, round, rectangular, oversized, wayfarer
4. **Frame Materials** — acetate, titanium, stainless, TR-90, wood, horn, mixed
5. **Vision Conditions** — myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, presbyopia, dry eye, glaucoma
6. **Eye Exams & Diagnostics** — comprehensive exam, OCT, visual field, retinal imaging
7. **Contact Lenses** — daily, monthly, toric, multifocal, scleral, colored
8. **Sunglasses & UV** — polarized, photochromic, sports, fashion, UV ratings
9. **Children's Vision** — myopia control, pediatric exams, school screening, durability
10. **Insurance & Buying** — Alberta coverage, employer plans, online vs in-store, fitting
